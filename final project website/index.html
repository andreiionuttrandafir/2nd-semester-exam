A Gingi Costume Company covers film and advertisement production.

A wide range of services are covered from costume design to costume Supervision all the way to providing a full costume department.

Our professional & coherent team doesn’t know the impossible.

Our new downtown studio is the perfect provider for any film or photo shoot.

A Gingi Costume Company provides a fully equipped location for stylist and costum designer clients. In our studio everything is given to construct costumes, sewing any classic piece to historical costumes.

We also rent out clothes from our extensive collection.

If you are only looking for a stylish and professional space for a fitting - we can provide an all-round solution.

We provide „On-set Duty” as well as „Emergency Duty” during film and ad shoots. Gingi Costume Company is ready to overcome any problem that can be solved with a sewing machine or two, within unrealistically short period of time.

Perfectly fitting dress gives such self-confidence that everyone should experience. Genji costume company is committed to make dreams come true, providing all-round services from design to creating individual pieces. The repertoire is wide and includes wedding dresses, full dresses, evening gownsas well as tail coats, dress suits, white tie and black tie.

Let it be one piece or dressing up a whole family, mending a dress or creating something entirely new the outcome is always guaranteed, our costumers are proudly wearing their tailor made clothes.

A Gingi Costume Company is a wardrobe expert and sylist for bands. What does that mean?

Creating and developing personal style, uniform concept and a full wardrobe. This includes buying, producing, renting for different occasions, ad hoc stylist consultancy, wardrobe review, dressing for concerts, also storing clothes accessories and maintenence.

Our wardrobe is open for anyone to browse through and rent for a day or a week.

A wide range of costumes and accessories are available.
